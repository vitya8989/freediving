AIDA 2 Freediver
No experience required

This course for beginners focuses on the correct breath-holding, relaxation techniques, safety guidelines, and equalization methods. During 5 water sessions participants learn to dive to a maximum depth of 12 meters. The course finishes with theoretical and practical exams to prove understanding of the theory and diver’s ability to apply the acquired skills in a safe and controlled manner.


The AIDA 2 Freediver course provides a solid foundation for further progression in freediving and opens the door to AIDA 3.

3 days

AIDA 3 Advanced Freediver

The AIDA 3 course builds upon the skills and knowledge acquired in previous levels and focuses on further developing your abilities as a freediver. It aims to enhance your safety, comfort, and performance in the water through a combination of theory and 6 practical sessions. The maximum depth for this level is typically around 24 to 30 meters.


This course will take you to the next level with a great reward of being one with your mind and body.
