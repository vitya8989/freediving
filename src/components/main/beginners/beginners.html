AIDA 1 discover Freediving
No experience required
It is the first level of freediving in the AIDA training system. Complete this freediving program for beginners and get licensed to dive to a max depth of 8 m. Get basic skills and knowledge of diving on a single breath, learn the most important safety procedures and enjoy wonderful marine landscapes of La Lajita bay at the same time. The training is conducted according to the standards of AIDA organization. Our professional instructor will guide you through a day full of adventures and make sure that you are ready to freedive with a buddy.
