about us

Fuerteventura is famous for its rich underwater world, clear waters and perfect conditions for freediving training. Our school Diving Atlantis located in a picturesque bay of La Lajita in the South of Fuerteventura is happy to help both beginners and experienced freedivers in mastering this exciting kind of sport. We share with each client our passion for the sea and enjoyable freediving, while safety is always our top priority.


Join us and go deep into the underwater world of Fuerteventura with its diverse marine life and volcanic sea bottom! You may encounter colorful fish such as parrotfish, damselfish, angelfish, and moray eels. Larger species like barracudas, groupers, rays and turtles can also be spotted, adding to the excitement of the freedive.


Big range of dive spots with different depth allows us to offer freediving sessions for any level.

freediving courses

Learn and develop the skills necessary for safe and enjoyable freediving experiences
AIDA 2 Freediver
No experience required

This course for beginners focuses on the correct breath-holding, relaxation techniques, safety guidelines, and equalization methods. During 5 water sessions participants learn to dive to a maximum depth of 12 meters. The course finishes with theoretical and practical exams to prove understanding of the theory and diver’s ability to apply the acquired skills in a safe and controlled manner.


The AIDA 2 Freediver course provides a solid foundation for further progression in freediving and opens the door to AIDA 3.

how to book freediving in fuerteventura

Booking freediving in Fuerteventura is so simple

Press the ¨Book with 10% discount¨ button and you will find a simple form where you choose the activity, day and hour of the tour and some other additional options and then you fill in a short formular in the end of which you click the link to pay with a card.

If you have a problem with finishing the booking online, please let us know by email or phone call.

Be noted

A booking of freediving in Fuerteventura can be made not less than 6 hours before the start of the activity.

If you stay in a private apartment, you need to send us its exact address (and pin on the map if possible) and on the day of the freediving tour, please, wait for the transfer outside of the building.

Writing your contact telephone number, remember to indicate the code of your country.

After finishing the booking, you will receive an automatic confirmation without pick up time. Shortly we will send you another email with the exact pick up hour. If you don't receive this second email from us on the same day, please, contact us.

If you would like to book last minute, please, contact us by phone or whatsapp.

If you stay in the North of Fuerteventura, you can reach our freediving school by car. We are located in La Lajita (https://goo.gl/maps/Uoy5AVDfrfDNHsVn9, see the map below).

Cancelation policy

50% refund if the tour is canceled 24 hours or earlier before the start of the tour.

0% refund if the tour is canceled less than 24 hours before the start of the tour.
